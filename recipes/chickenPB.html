Chicken Pasta Bake

Chicken breast and penne pasta with Leggos finest sauce, all topped with mozzarella cheese.

This is very basic meal that requires very few ingredients and is enjoyed by all walks of life. A quick and easy dinner to feed the family.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken breast
  • 3 cups penne pasta
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 1 jar of Leggos pasta sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees Celsius
  2. Bring a large pot of water to the boil and cook penne pasta for 15 minutes
  3. While cooking the pasta, heat a large saucepan with a dash of oil and cook the diced chicken breast for roughly 12 minutes
  4. Strain the pasta and combine with the chicken
  5. Add the sauce and mix well, top with mozzarella cheese
  6. Place in an ovenproof dish and pop it in the oven for 20 minutes
